Albrecht Dürer @ the Met




This is a quantative sprint representing Albrecht Dürer's works in The Metropolitan Museum of Art in Manhattan, New York. The data visualization juxtaposes Dürer's life along with what pieces of his art are housed in The Met based on the object create date.

Düring German Renaissance @ the Met




This is a quantative sprint representing works in The Metropolitan Museum of Art in Manhattan, New York, during the German Renaissance with the focus on Albrecht Dürer. The sprint juxtaposes Wikipedia data, along with The Met public domain images that transpire for the object create dates of Albrecht Dürer's works of art.
